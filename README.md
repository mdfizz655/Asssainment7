# 🌿 KeenKeeper - Nurture Your Friendships

**KeenKeeper** is a simple yet powerful personal relationship manager designed to help you stay connected with the people who matter most. In our busy daily lives, it's easy to lose track of time and forget to check in on friends. This app acts as your personal "shelf" of connections, reminding you when it's time to reach out and keep those bonds strong.


## 📖 Project Overview
This project was built as part of a React assignment to demonstrate core concepts like **Context API**, **React Router**, **State Management**, and **Data Visualization**. The goal was to create a clean, user-friendly dashboard that tracks interaction history and relationship goals.

### Why I built this:
- To visually track which friends need attention (Overdue vs. On-track).
- To maintain a timeline of every call, text, or video hangout.
- To understand my social habits through simple analytics.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- **🎯 Color-Coded Dashboard:** A quick glance at the home page shows you who is "Overdue" for a chat and who is "On-track" based on your personal contact goals.
- **⚡ Quick Check-Ins:** No more manual logging! With one click (Call, Text, or Video), the app automatically records the interaction, adds it to your timeline, and notifies you with a toast.
- **📊 Friendship Analytics:** A dedicated stats page using interactive Pie Charts to help you see how you mostly interact with your circle.
- **📜 Interaction Timeline:** A historical log of every conversation you've recorded, filterable by interaction type so you can look back at your social history.
